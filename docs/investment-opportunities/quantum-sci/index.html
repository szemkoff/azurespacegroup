Quantum for Science Initiative (QUANTUM-SCI)

Overview

The Quantum for Science Initiative (QUANTUM-SCI) is a major funding opportunity supporting groundbreaking research in quantum technologies and their applications in both fundamental science and practical engineering implementations.

Eligibility

  • Research institutions (universities, national labs)
  • Small businesses with quantum technology focus
  • Non-profit organizations advancing quantum research

Grant Amount

$50,000 - $2,000,000 per project, dependent on scope and potential impact

Application Components

  • Detailed research proposal (max 15 pages)
  • Budget justification and timeline
  • Team qualifications and expertise
  • Letters of support from partnering organizations
